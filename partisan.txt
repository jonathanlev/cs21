Dataset:  partisan.dat

Source: J.M. Clubb and Santa A. Traugatt (1977), "Partisan Cleavage
and Cohesion in the House of Representatives, 1861-1974",
Journal of Interdisciplinary History, Vol 7, #3, pp375-401.

Description: Party Unlikeness Scores and % Party Votes for each
Congress 1861-1974

Variables/Columns

Congress #   7-8
Beginning Year    13-16
Average Party Unlikeness   21-24
% Party Votes          31-32


NOTE: Party UnLikeness = Max(Party1%For,Party2%For)-Min(Party1%For,Party2%For)
% Party Votes = % of Votes where majority of one party votes one way,
majority of other party votes other way
